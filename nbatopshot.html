Connect with NBA Top Shot

This page was inspired by NBA Top Shot. NBA Top Shot is a community where users can buy and sell moments from the NBA. Top Shot is licensed by the NBA which allows regular people to own an NBA moment. The following are a list of features that are available on NBA Top Shot.

1. Collect your favorite players

NBA Top Shot allows regular people to collect your favorite player, rookie, or team by opening a pack or browsing the marketplace.

2. Verified and authentic moments

Each collectible is marked with a unique serial number when created. Every moment you own is your own unique moment.

3. Show off your moments

The showcase feature in NBA Top Shot allows users to showcase their favorite moments to the rest of the Top Shot community.

4. Explore

NBA Top Shot does not favor one collecting strategy over another. Find out what strategy be suits you by collecting your first moment.
